About Me.

I am Ihsan-Ahmad Olamide Sodeko, a software developer with a unique journey from First-Class Marine Biologist to full-stack problem solver as an engineer. I specialize in building scalable solutions with .NET, C#, and JavaScript, blending scientific precision with a creative edge. My background spans environmental science, data systems, and leadership, which fuels my passion for clean code, sustainable thinking, and user-centered design. I’ve led initiatives that impacted thousands of young people across various local governments in Lagos, interned in sectors like weather forecasting, tourism, and public sanitation, and delivered tech projects ranging from banking simulations to task management tools. Beyond the keyboard, I’m a youth leader, speaker, and digital storyteller while I'm also in my workspace debugging backend logic. Whether I’m building apps, managing youth programs, or crafting a clear narrative, I’m driven by one goal: to build with purpose and lead with impact.
